About

Like Monet's water lilies that bloomed endlessly in his garden at Giverny, I seek the beauty in everyday moments—the way morning light trembles on water, how shadows paint their soft edges across familiar spaces.

My work is an exploration of atmosphere and feeling, capturing not what the eye sees, but what the heart remembers. Each piece is an impression, a whisper of a moment suspended in time.
